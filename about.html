01 / Origin

Started with questions,
built from curiosity.

Born and raised in Kerala, I’ve always been curious about how things work — why something feels intuitive, why something doesn’t, and what makes an experience memorable.

That curiosity slowly turned into an interest in design, where I began exploring how visuals, interactions, and structure come together to shape the way people experience things.

Today, I see design as a way to understand people better and build solutions that actually make a difference.

02 / Education

NIFT Kannur sharpened
the craft.

I’m currently a 3rd year Communication Design student at the National Institute of Fashion Technology, Kannur. Over the past three years, I’ve had the chance to explore different mediums and perspectives.

This journey has shaped how I think — understanding how ideas take form and how small decisions impact the overall experience.

I approach learning by experimenting, refining, and continuously improving.

04 / Beyond design

Life apart design.

Sports have always been a big part of my life. Along with basketball, shot put has been something I’ve pursued seriously over the years. I had the opportunity to compete at the SGFI meet in Sangrur, Punjab, representing Kerala — an experience that pushed me both physically and mentally.

Currently, I’m part of the basketball and athletics teams at the National Institute of Fashion Technology, Kannur.

Being around sports has shaped the way I think — discipline, consistency, and constantly working to improve. Outside of playing, I closely follow the NBA, along with football and athletics.

I also spend time writing my thoughts and exploring photography, capturing moments that often go unnoticed.
